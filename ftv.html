    • Seamless Fire TV Navigation
    Enhancing User Experience Through IA Design
    Building a new IA and navigation for Fire TV, improving content discovery and navigation across content types.
    My role
    UX design
    Systems design
    Timeline
    Q3 2024 - Ongoing
    Team
    3 designers, 1 design technologist, 1 UX researcher, 1 product manager
    Problem
    The current Fire TV IA is makes it difficult for customers to find what they are looking for and does not scale for Fire TV's ever-growing content and category needs (e.g. games, music, and more).
    This project aims to create an IA that delivers a simple and clear customer experience while enabling business growth and showcasing all offerings on Fire TV.
    What I did
    Object Model and IA:
    Developed a system-wide object model to define content relationships and user flows, establishing the IA foundation.
    Prototypes & RITE testing:
    Created prototypes and improved designs through Rapid Iterative Testing and Evaluation with Fire TV customers.
    Audit & Remote Mapping:
    Optimized backstack behavior remote button mapping for consistent user interactions.
    Browse Patterns:
    Redesigned browse patterns to enhance content organization and accessibility.
