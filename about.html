    • Nice to meet you - I'm Janet. 👋
    I’m a multidisciplinary UX designer currently creating experiences for Fire TV at Amazon. Previously, I contributed to the Alexa Design System, where I led large-scale, multimodal projects that spanned voice, touch, and screenless interactions.
    While my design career began at Amazon, my passion for the field was sparked at Berkeley, where I pursued degrees in cognitive and data science and helped design a student-run course enrollment site that quickly became a favorite among students. Since then, I've cultivated diverse experiences spanning conversational AI and LLM technology, developing scalable design systems and frameworks, and designing consumer entertainment platforms.
    Throughout it all, my strong systems-thinking background has served me well in understanding complex systems and designing products that integrate seamlessly into larger environments.
    In my free time, you’ll find me dedicating an absurd amount of hours to crafting 3D environments and eating an equally absurd amount of breads, buns, and tortillas.
