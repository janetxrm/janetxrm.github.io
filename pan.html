    • Palo Alto Networks Internship

    Summer of 2019 I had the opportunity to intern at Palo Alto Networks in their stunning Santa Clara headquarters. I was a UX/UI intern in the Customer Experience Department.

     

    WHAT I DID

    I worked on consumer facing project of designing the customer support portal for both internal customer support employees and Palo Alto Networks customers. I worked closely with a PM and a handful of engineers to improve the usability and effectiveness of PAN's customer support portal and to decrease the number of tickets being open.

     

    MAIN LEARNINGS

    From working cross-functionally with others, I learned how to prioritize ideas and how to approach engineering contraints, finding it especially helpful to hold workshops to do so early on. I was also able to conduct internal interviews with security operations services users and sales engineers, learning that a lot of invaluable insights can be gained regardless of whether or not you have direct access to customers.
