    • Palo Alto Networks Internship

    Summer of 2019 I had the opportunity to intern at Palo Alto Networks in their stunning Santa Clara headquarters.

    What I Did

    I worked on an end to end project of designing the customer support portal for both internal customer support employees and Palo Alto Networks customers. I worked closely with a PM and a handful of engineers to improve the usability of PAN's customer support portal and to increase its engagement rates.

    Project Roadmap

    While I can't disclose the full details of my project, I can share a high level roadmap that I followed throughout the course of my internship.

    Synthesized the design findings made by a contractor previously hired for designing the support portal

    Held a cross-functional workshop to define the user problem, prioritize features, and discuss engineering contraints

    Conducted internal interviews with security operations services users and sales engineers

    Ideated design solutions given engineering constraints

    Aligned a high fidelity mockup with our department's design system

    Presented work at a company wide "Internfest "

    If you're interested in learning more about this project, feel free to reach out to me and I'd be happy to chat more about it!

     

    Special thanks to my manager Mike and the entire Customer Experience team for such a memorable and fulfilling summer.
