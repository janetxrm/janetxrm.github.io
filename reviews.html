    Review Insights at a Glance
    Designed a review insights system and defined how it surfaces across product pages and search.
    My role
    Product design owner
    Duration
    4 months
    Team
    1 product manager, 2 developers
    BEFORE
    Problem
    Buried insights
    Review content is dense and unstructured. Shoppers waste time hunting for relevant feedback.
    No comparison
    On the search results page, users must open multiple pages to compare items as each lacks review insights.
    Design principles
    Insights first vs top down reading
    Show the most meaningful review insights up front so users can understand product performance without scrolling.
    Support quick comparison
    Enable shoppers to compare items efficiently by surfacing insights in search and reducing the need to open multiple pages.
    Scale across categories
    Design modular insights that work across product types and edge cases, including items with low review volume.
    SOLUTION 1
    AI-generated review summary
    Reveal insight details
    The summary highlights most common review themes and expands to full insight descriptions.
    Filter by theme
    Users select an insight to filter reviews to matching feedback.
    SOLUTION 2
    Insight-first reviews
    Feature ratings
    Give users an immediate signal of how a product performs in key areas.
    Fit insights
    Shows how an item fits based on customer feedback.
    SOLUTION 3
    Category-driven information hierarchy
    Visuals first
    Product visuals are elevated to match their higher engagement for apparel.
    Visuals deprioritized
    Review insights are surfaced at the top of the page for faster understanding.
    SOLUTION 4
    Signals in search
    Insights on hover
    Reveals review signals on hover so users can preview insights instantly.
    Insights on tap
    Opens a bottom sheet to show key review insights for quick comparison.
    DESIGNED TO SCALE
    Scaling one system across categories
    Impact
    The results a few months out from rolling out these updates were:
    +10% ▼
    Click-through rate on the newly surfaced review summary module
    -10%
    Reduced scroll time in the reviews section due to improved IA
