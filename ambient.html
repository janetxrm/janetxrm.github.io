    Overview
    I work on personal projects inspired by my favorite fandoms, where I 3D model environments and use Foley techniques to record and pair audio with visuals.
    Through extensive experimentation and post-production, I create immersive worlds that are my own take on scenes from my favorite fandoms.
    This particular project is inspired by the movie Coraline - one of my childhood favorites.
