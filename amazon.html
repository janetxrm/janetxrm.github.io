    • Amazon Internship

    I spent the summer of 2020 as a UX design intern on Amazon's Alexa Core CX team. I learned about creating touch and voice forward experiences on Alexa Echo Show devices, and worked with motion and sound designers in working towards my final deliverable.

     

    WHAT I DID

    My project focus was to push the customization, visual, and CX expression of the idle state displayed on Alexa Echo Show devices. My final deliverable is a touch and voice forward experience that implements collaborated work done with sound designers, motion designers, and illustrators, and that scales across different device models and generations.

     

    MAIN LEARNINGS

    There was no clear path for how to tackle my project so it was important to iterate effectively, over communicate, and share ideas early and often. This led to productive design feedback sessions and team visibility to my project.

    This was also my first time working in a product and voice driven space, so I learned a lot about it through user research, product needs, and 1:1's with other members of Amazon's Device Design Group.
